ABOUT SOME OF THE MAJOR CRYPTOCURRENCIES

Bitcoin:

Bitcoin, often referred to as digital gold, is the first and most well-known cryptocurrency. It was created in 2009 by an unknown person or group using the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto. Bitcoin operates on a decentralized peer-to-peer network, allowing for secure and transparent transactions. It is widely used as a store of value and a medium of exchange.

Ethereum:

Ethereum is a decentralized platform that enables the creation and execution of smart contracts and decentralized applications (DApps). Launched in 2015, Ethereum introduced the concept of "smart contracts," self-executing contracts with the terms of the agreement directly written into code. Ether (ETH) is the native cryptocurrency of the Ethereum platform.
